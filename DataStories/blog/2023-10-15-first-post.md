This is my first post, how exciting!

Blogging about science, technology and computing has always been on my to-do list for a long time. I always believed that this can be an exciting and rewarding experience for a number of reasons. For me, the main reason is the opportunity to share my passion for these topics with others and to contribute to the ongoing conversation around them.  

As someone who has always been fascinated by the latest developments in science and technology, I find blogging to be a great way to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and innovations. By researching and writing about these topics, I will be able to deepen my own understanding and share that knowledge with others. I can definitely learn quite a lot about these topics and as Richard Feymann once said "if you want to master something, teach it".  

This space will be dedicated to my own reflactions about the latest news in science, my take into technological developments or some computing resources. I will try to look into hacks and tutorials for linux or embedded systems that I learn while working on personal projects. 

Finally, I find that blogging about my progress and experiences is a great way to hold myself accountable and track my progress. By sharing my struggles and successes with others, I hope to not only help others, but also to solidify my own understanding of the different topics.
